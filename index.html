about us

WHO WE ARE

New-Wine Innovation is birthed on the basic principles of merging varieties of organizations ranging from charitable to business organizations (mostly startups) to pull ideas collectively and achieve a global goal of influencing world activities through various merged start-ups and other existing organizations via our collective ideas. Founded on May 2, 2018 by Daniel Adeniran, a student of Covenant University, New Wine Innovation likened to Warren Buffett’s Berkshire Hathaway is called an Innovation because its approach to winning organizations (starting from Covenant University of course), to be under the umbrella of the New Wine is the “Idea-barter” approach. This is simply making them sell-in through ideas, innovations and strategies to expanding their organizations via New Wine Innovation.

The Word “New Wine” refers to “a divine approach which has never been used”, this was brought up from John2:10 AMP which says “Everyone else serves his best wine first, and when people have drunk freely, then he serves that which is not so good, but you have kept back the good wine until now”.

New Wine Innovation operates on fundamentals known as the IDEAS
I - Intuition
D - Diligence/ Dedication
E - Experience/ Exposure
A - Analytics
S - Spirituality; which is the foundation, thrust and driver force of every IDEA.

WHAT IS OUR TARGET GOAL?

New Wine Innovation is a commission concerned with making sure all global activities from charitable to business activities have its running processes by New-wine Innovation thereby having significant strategies to achieving common goals and working together to achieve them. This is just in accordance to making sure global activities are in line with God’s own plan and not of the world.
It’s been said, “Two heads are better than one” but we say “Both heads must agree to make themselves better”. This is going to be the greatest avenue were businesses and non-business activities of set goals reason and move forward.

WHAT HAVE WE BEEN UP TO?

Since establishment, New-Wine Innovation has been mainly a philanthropic organization and as therefore embarked on multiple outreaches on “The community service; cloth drive” and “The book drive”.
We are currently working on an association with another charitable organization and a business organization and via pulling together ideas and experiences, we move farther and closer to our set goal.

WHAT IS OUR DRIVING FORCE?

Our MISSION is to influence world activities through divine inspiration and ideas thereby acquiring ventures to become world class organizations that will add real value and impact lives globally.

OUR ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE

Every organization works with a defined structure and so therefore, our structure is pretty much defined.

We have just 2 units which oversee the administration and execution of projects

  • The Operations unit and
  • The Finance unit

The foreman of the organization and other administrators are under the Operations unit where all Administrations, Research and developments, projects and programs execution are checked and acted upon.
The Finance unit is controlled by the Operations unit and therefore any monetary activities of New wine Innovation follow due policies and processes. This unit has its own executives who make certain financial decisions as agreed upon by the top executives.
